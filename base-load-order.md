<details>
<summary>📖 Reading Tips (Click to expand)</summary>

A typical `loadorder.txt` only lists **plugins** (files ending in `.esm`, `.esp`, or `.esl`) found on the right-hand panel of Mod Organizer 2 (MO2). However, some mods do not have a plugin file at all, some use multiple files, and others are named something entirely different from their actual plugins. 

To make things as clear as possible, this list provides:

1️⃣ **Official Mod Name & Link:** So you know exactly what to download.

2️⃣ **Mod Categories:** Arranged by how they live in the left-hand pane of MO2, so you can organise your mods to perfection.

3️⃣ **Plugin Position:** Showing exactly where active plugins land in MO2's  right-side panel.

I've also added commentary on why I've made certain choices where it's helpful, like highlighting mods that don't currently support the Anniversary Edition update, features I personally skip, or more stable alternatives.

Okay, enough preamble. <b><i>Allons-y!</i></b>
</details>
